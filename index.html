  6. Perfumes


    1.Sample the fragrance on a sample card to determine if it’s your style, but don’t ever make a final selection based on a card.
    2.Note that the same fragrance can smell very different on different people. Make sure you try it on yourself .
    3. Avoid wearing other fragranced products (shower gels, lotions) so that you can smell the fragrance in an unadulterated state.
